facaccia with caramelized onions, cranberriers, and brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia aspetizer topped with sweet caramelizzed onions, tart cranberriers, and craemy brie. it will become your new faavorite holiday starter!

featured in:

fresh crnberry recipes that go beyond sauce

ingredients

for the focaccia:

. 4 cups (500g)all-purpose flour
. 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
. 11/2 teaspoons salt
. 13/4 cups (350g) lukewrm water
. 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
for the topping:
. 3 medium yellow onions thinly sliced
. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
. 1-2 tablespoon olive oil for brushing on top
. 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired,though it's not necessary)
. 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberriers
Method
1 Make the fogaccia dough

in a medium sized mixing bowl, or in stand mixer with a paddel attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead the dough though I sometimes it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporete the last of the flour...
